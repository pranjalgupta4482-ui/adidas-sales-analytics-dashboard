# Adidas Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Adidas sales performance using data analytics and visualization tools.
The objective is to uncover key business insights related to revenue trends, product performance, regional sales distribution, and future revenue forecasting.

The project demonstrates the use of **data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, and interactive dashboard creation** to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Retail companies need to understand:

* Which products generate the highest revenue
* Which regions perform best in sales
* How sales trends change over time
* What future revenue may look like based on historical patterns

This project helps answer these questions using analytics and visualization.

## Key Dashboard Features

### 1. Sales Overview Dashboard

Provides a high-level view of overall sales performance.

Key Metrics:

* Total Revenue
* Total Units Sold
* Average Revenue per Unit
* Total Orders

Visuals:

* KPI Cards
* Revenue Trend by Date
* Category-wise Revenue
* Sales Distribution

---

### 2. Product Performance Analysis

Analyzes how different products contribute to revenue.

Insights:

* Top performing products
* SKU-wise revenue contribution
* Product category comparison
* Top 10 products by revenue

---

### 3. Regional Performance Analysis

Identifies geographical sales performance.

Insights:

* Revenue by region
* Region-wise sales contribution
* High-performing markets
* Sales distribution across regions

---

### 4. Sales Forecasting

Predicts future revenue trends based on historical data.

Forecast Analysis Includes:

* Projected revenue trends
* Expected sales growth patterns
* Demand stability indicators
* Future sales planning insights

---

## Key Insights

* Certain product categories contribute significantly to total revenue.
* A few regions dominate overall sales performance.
* Revenue trends show seasonal fluctuations.
* Forecast results suggest stable revenue growth in upcoming periods.

## Project Outcome

This project demonstrates how data analytics can transform raw sales data into actionable insights that support strategic business decisions.
